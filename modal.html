Cat

Porcelain pieces, 14" x 12" x 7", 2020

My cat is very naughty, always jumping up and down in the house. Several beautiful vases were broken by her. Staring at the shatter porcelain fragments, I suddenly had a wonderful idea. I put the pieces together in an image of a cat. Then I colored each fragment according to its shape and created a unique sculpture.

A Rainy Night

Color pencil, 10" x 14", 2022

The pitter-patter rain falling at nightfall. The reflected headlights and street lamps in the rain covered the road with a warm yellow, contrasting it with the cold sky. Pedestrians were all walking in a hurry. It is the most common scene in the city. As a filter lens, the rain blurred the pedestrians and cars into a perfect impressionist painting, adding vibrant color to a bustling city. Touched by this scene, I used colored pencils to jot it down with disordered lines.
