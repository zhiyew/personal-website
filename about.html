About

Biography

Zhiye Wang(b.2003), is an artist from Hangzhou, China. Studied in University of Michigan Ann Arbor Stamps School of Art & Design.

Artist Statement

My work explores the connection of people and nature and childhood memory. I like to experiment with many different materials like pencil, acrylic, paper, acrylic plate. I want to deliver a sense of vigour and warmth through my artwork.
